### Inspiration
I wanted to make a macro pad to help me quickly open up my AI assistants. So, I created a macro pad to quickly open up ChatGPT, Gemini, Cursor, and DeepSeek.

### Specifications

BOM: 
- 4x Cherry MX Switches
- 2x SK6812 MINI Leds
- 1x XIAO RP2040
- 4x Blank DSA Keycaps
- 4x M3x14 Bolt
- 4x M3 Heatset

Others:
- KMK Firmware
